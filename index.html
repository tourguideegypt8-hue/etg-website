Our Projects

Egypt Tour Guide

Egypt Tour Guide is a mobile application designed to help users explore the most famous tourist attractions in Egypt.

  • 📍 Location
  • 🎟 Ticket Prices
  • 📖 History
  • 🖼 Photos

It is especially useful for students, tourists, and anyone interested in learning more about Egypt’s rich cultural heritage.

⭐ Main Features:

  • 🏛 Tourist Places: A list of the top 20 tourist attractions in Egypt.
  • 📍 Location Details: Each place includes its exact location and a map link.
  • 🎟 Price Information: Ticket prices for adults, students, and foreigners.
  • 🖼 Images & Description: Photos and historical background of each site.
